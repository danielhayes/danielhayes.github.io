My Dog Boris, LLC is Dan Hayes, independent senior enterprise architect and software engineer specializing in the design and development of highly scalable web applications and micro services deployed both on premise and via cloud services.

Over the past 12 years, my work has been focused primarily within the media/cable industry, where I play an important role in the modernization of various important applications and platforms for Comcast NBC Universal. Prior to Comcast, I consulted to numerous companies in financial services, insurance, supply chain and e-commerce industries. In addition, I served as an interim/turnaround CIO/CTO for companies such as JG Wentworth and Storeroom Solutions. Prior to focusing on exclusively on technology, I was an insurance executive with Progressive Insurance and co-founder of internet startup insurer Kemper Direct Auto and Home which was later acquired by Unitrin Direct.

As a Certified Enterprise JAVA Architect for over 20 years, I am primarily called upon to assist companies who have a bias toward Java-based solutions. As a result, Spring and derivative technologies (such as Spring Boot, Spring Integration, Spring Data, etc.) have been central to my professional toolkit. However, as a full-stack architect and developer, I am also well versed in UI application design and development based upon Javascript frameworks such as Vue and Svelte/SvelteKit. I have also designed numerous APIs and micro services that leverage NodeJS, most of which are deployed within an AWS cloud environment.
